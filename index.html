Introduction

This small building built about 30 years ago is the first generation house made with new construction materials in our country. The house of that generation is losing popularity and is beginning to disappear. This house was also getting old and planned to demolish. However, this house is what Tomoyuki's grandmother built at the end of her life. When he heard about the story of demolition, he was very sad. He fixed this small, old house. And successfully passed the current standards set by the country.
